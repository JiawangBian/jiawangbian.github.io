Research Interests:

  • Spatial AI
  • World Models
  • Multimodal Models

I will join Nanyang Technological University (NTU) as a Nanyang Assistant Professor in the College of Computing and Data Science in May 2026. My research focuses on Spatial AI, world models, and multimodal models. I aim to bridge 3D computer vision and next-generation AI systems that understand and interact with the physical world.

Currently, I am a Research Scientist at Bytedance Seed (since April 2025). Previously, I was a postdoctoral researcher at MBZUAI (2024-2025) working with Prof. Ian Reid, and at the University of Oxford (2022-2023) working with Prof. Philip Torr. I received my Ph.D. from the University of Adelaide in 2022, supervised by Prof. Ian Reid and Prof. Chunhua Shen, and my B.Eng. from Nankai University in 2016, supervised by Prof. Ming-Ming Cheng.

To Prospective Students:

I am looking for highly motivated PhD students, Postdocs, Research Assistants, and Visiting Students to join my group at NTU starting in 2026. If you are interested in Spatial AI, world models, multimodal learning, or 3D computer vision, please send me an email with your CV.
